About Pixel Phantoms

"From Students, For Students."

A fully student-driven tech community focused on peer-to-peer learning and growth.

Who We Are

Pixel Phantoms is a student-driven tech club focused on hands-on learning, collaboration, and real-world projects in web development, programming, design, embedded systems, and open source. We are a community-driven learning platform, not a company or paid program.

Who is this for?

Beginners to advanced students interested in building real projects together, collaborating with peers, and growing their technical skills in a supportive community environment.
