Community Guidelines

This document defines the expected behavior for all members, contributors, and maintainers of Pixel Phantoms.

Our Commitment

We, as contributors and maintainers, pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

  • Being respectful and considerate
  • Using welcoming and inclusive language
  • Giving constructive feedback
  • Focusing on what is best for the community
  • Showing empathy towards others

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

  • Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
  • Trolling or creating a hostile environment
  • Publishing others’ private information without permission
  • Discriminatory comments or actions
  • Disruptive or aggressive behavior

Our Responsibilities

  • Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
  • Taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior
  • Removing or rejecting contributions that violate this Code of Conduct
  • Temporarily or permanently banning contributors for severe violations

Scope

  • Within all project-related spaces
  • In public spaces when representing the project or its community

Enforcement Guidelines

  1. Warning – Verbal or written warning for minor violations.
  2. Temporary Ban – Temporary removal from interactions.
  3. Permanent Ban – Severe or repeated violations.
